# Frosthold

## Overview
The ancestral castle of House Northwind and capital of the Northern Reaches.

## Description
Built over natural hot springs, Frosthold remains warm even during the harshest winters. Its walls are thick granite, and its towers have stood for a thousand years.

## Notable Features
- **The Great Hall**: Where the northern lords gather to pledge allegiance
- **The Godswood**: Ancient grove with a heart tree at its center
- **The Crypts**: Burial place of Northwind lords for generations
