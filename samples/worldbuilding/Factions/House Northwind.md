# House Northwind

## Overview

The ancient Great House of the Northern Reaches, ruling from [[Frosthold]] for thousands of years. Known for their honor, resilience, and connection to the old ways. Their sigil is a grey wolf on a white field.

## Leadership

Lord [[Aldric Northwind]], Warden of the North and Lord of Frosthold.

## Goals and Motivations

- Protect the realm from threats beyond the Frostwall
- Uphold family honor and duty
- Maintain the independence and traditions of the North

## History

Founded in the Age of Heroes by Brennan the Builder, who also constructed the Frostwall and Frosthold. The Northwinds ruled as Kings in the North for thousands of years until Torren Northwind bent the knee to the Dragonlords.

## Relationships

- Allied with [[House Riverdale]] through Lord Aldric's marriage to Catrin
- Allied with [[House Mountainkeep]] through Aldric's fostering there
- Rivals of [[House Goldmane]] and [[House Redfort]]

## Resources

- **Frosthold**: Ancient seat with hot spring heating
- **The Northern Reaches**: Largest region by area
- **Northern Lords**: Loyal bannermen including Umbers, Ironhills, Bearclaws

## Notes

Words: "Winter Endures" - both a warning and a promise of Northwind resilience.
