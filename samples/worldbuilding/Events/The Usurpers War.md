# The Usurper's War

## Summary

A civil war that ended the Dragonlord dynasty and placed Duke Roderick Stormvale on the throne. The war began after Crown Prince Vaelen allegedly abducted Lady Lyra Northwind, and King Maegor executed Lord Rickard Northwind and his heir Brandon.

## Participants

- [[Lord Aldric Northwind]] - Led northern forces alongside Roderick
- [[Duke Roderick Stormvale]] - Leader of the rebellion, betrothed to Lyra Northwind

## Timeline

- **282 ER**: War begins after Lyra Northwind's disappearance and the execution of Rickard and Brandon Northwind
- **283 ER**: Battle of the Crimson Ford - Roderick slays Prince Vaelen
- **283 ER**: Fall of the Capital - The Mad King meets his end

## Outcome

Duke Roderick Stormvale crowned King of the Realm. The Dragonlord children flee across the sea.

## Long-term Impact

- End of 300 years of Dragonlord rule
- New political alliances formed through marriage
- Seeds of future conflict planted

## Notes

One of the defining events of recent history. The true circumstances of Lyra's "abduction" remain disputed.
