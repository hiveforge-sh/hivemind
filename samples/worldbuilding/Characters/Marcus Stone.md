# Marcus Stone

Marcus Stone is a veteran soldier and Elara's most trusted companion. A former captain of the [[Tidewatch]] city guard, he left his post to help Elara on her quest after she saved his life during a demon attack.

## Background

Marcus grew up in the slums of [[Tidewatch]], working his way up through the city guard through sheer determination and skill. His distrust of magic stems from a childhood incident where a rogue mage destroyed his family's home.

## Skills

- Master swordsman
- Expert tactician
- Skilled in defensive combat
- Natural leader

## Relationships

- **[[Elara Brightwood]]** - His closest friend, though he often worries about her recklessness
- **[[Captain Lyra]]** - His replacement in the city guard, and a romantic interest
