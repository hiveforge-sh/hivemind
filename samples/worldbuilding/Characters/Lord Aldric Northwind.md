# Lord Aldric Northwind

## Overview
Lord of Frosthold and Warden of the Northern Reaches.

## Background
Born at Frosthold, fostered at the Mountain Keep in his youth. Known for his quiet honor and unwavering sense of duty.

## Personality
Reserved and thoughtful, Aldric speaks rarely but carries great weight when he does. Values honor above political gain.

## Relationships
- Leader of [[House Northwind]]
- Ally of [[House Stormvale]] through old bonds
