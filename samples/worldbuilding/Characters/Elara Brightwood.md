# Elara Brightwood

Elara Brightwood is a powerful mage who specializes in stellar magic. Born in the coastal city of [[Tidewatch]], she discovered her abilities at age twelve when she accidentally summoned a meteor shower during a thunderstorm.

## Background

Raised by her grandmother after her parents disappeared during an expedition to the [[Shadowfell]], Elara grew up hearing stories of ancient magic and forgotten civilizations. These tales sparked her lifelong fascination with recovering lost magical knowledge.

## Current Goals

- Uncover the truth about her parents' disappearance
- Master the legendary Starweave technique
- Protect [[Tidewatch]] from the growing threat of the [[Shadow Court]]

## Relationships

- **[[Marcus Stone]]** - Her closest friend and fellow adventurer
- **[[The Arcane Academy]]** - Where she trained and now teaches
- **[[Grand Magister Theron]]** - Her mentor and father figure
