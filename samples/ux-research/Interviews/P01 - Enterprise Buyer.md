# Interview: P01 - Enterprise Buyer

## Participant Background

- Role: Procurement Specialist at a 500-person company
- Uses product for: Office supplies and equipment
- Purchase frequency: 2-3 orders per week
- Average order value: $1,200

## Key Observations

### Mobile Usage
- Uses mobile for browsing and product research
- **Always switches to desktop** for actual purchasing
- Cited approval workflows as main reason

### Checkout Pain Points
1. Cannot save cart for later approval
2. No way to share cart with approvers
3. Multi-step approval not supported
4. Receipt format doesn't meet finance requirements

### Quotes

> "I need to get approval before I can complete any purchase over $500. The app just doesn't support that."

> "I wish I could save carts and share them with my manager. Right now I screenshot everything and paste it into Slack."

> "The mobile app is useless for my workflow - I always switch to desktop for anything serious."

## Insights Derived

- [[Mobile checkout fails for enterprise approval workflows]]
- [[Cart sharing is a critical missing feature]]
