# Interview: P02 - Small Business Owner

## Participant Background

- Role: Owner of a small retail shop
- Uses product for: Restocking inventory
- Purchase frequency: Daily
- Average order value: $150

## Key Observations

### Mobile Usage
- **Mobile-first user** — does everything on phone
- No desktop computer at work location
- Quick, frequent purchases

### Checkout Experience
1. Generally satisfied with checkout flow
2. Frustrated by re-entering address
3. Would value saved payment methods
4. Interested in expedited delivery options

### Quotes

> "I do everything from my phone - I don't even have a computer at the shop."

> "The checkout is fine but entering my address every time is annoying. Why can't it just remember?"

> "I'd pay more if I could get same-day delivery. When I'm out of something, I need it NOW."

## Insights Derived

- [[SMB users are mobile-first and satisfied with basic checkout]]
- [[Address auto-fill is expected but missing]]
