# House Stark

## Overview

The ancient Great House of the North, ruling from [[Winterfell]] for thousands of years. Known for their honor, resilience, and connection to the old ways. Their sigil is a grey direwolf on a white field.

## Leadership

Lord [[Eddard Stark]], Warden of the North and Lord of Winterfell.

## Goals and Motivations

- Protect the realm from threats beyond the Wall
- Uphold family honor and duty
- Maintain the independence and traditions of the North

## History

Founded in the Age of Heroes by Bran the Builder, who also constructed the Wall and Winterfell. The Starks ruled as Kings in the North for thousands of years until Torrhen Stark bent the knee to Aegon the Conqueror.

## Relationships

- Allied with [[House Tully]] through Lord Eddard's marriage to Catelyn
- Allied with [[House Arryn]] through Eddard's fostering at the Eyrie
- Rivals of [[House Lannister]] and [[House Bolton]]

## Resources

- **Winterfell**: Ancient seat with hot spring heating
- **The North**: Largest of the Seven Kingdoms by area
- **Northern Lords**: Loyal bannermen including Umbers, Karstarks, Mormonts

## Notes

Words: "Winter is Coming" - both a warning and a promise of Stark endurance.
